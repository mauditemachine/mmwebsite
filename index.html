Maudite Machine, a producer and dj with a dark disco and indie dance style, captivates the crowds with his mystical energy. Based in Canada for 15 years, Maudite Machine has ignited the tracks of the biggest festivals such as Eclipse, Totem, Triskel, Illusion, Groove & Bass. His captivating performances in emblematic places such as the Daomé Salon and the Darling Foundry have taken his audience on a journey. Sharing the stage with influential DJs such as Agoria, Electric Rescue, Riva Star, Popof, Carl Craig and Perfect Stranger, Maudite Machine has established himself as a prolific electro artist with numerous electronic music EPs.
